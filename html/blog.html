10 Common Grammar Mistakes

Discover the most frequent errors writers make and how to fix them…

1. Subject-Verb Agreement

Ensure singular subjects have singular verbs and plural subjects have plural verbs.

2. Misplaced Modifiers

Modifiers should be placed next to the words they describe to avoid confusion.

3. Comma Splices

Do not join two independent clauses with only a comma. Use a semicolon or conjunction.
